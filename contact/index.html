Common Questions

What is the best way to contact you?
Email is the best way to reach us. We typically respond within 24-48 hours during business days.
Can you add more words to the dictionary?
We're always working to improve our word database. If you notice missing common Spanish words, please email us with specific examples and we'll review them for inclusion.
I found a bug. How do I report it?
Please email us at feedback@spanishwordunscrambler.com with details about the issue, including what browser you're using and what happened. Screenshots are helpful if possible.
Do you offer an API or bulk access?
Currently, we don't offer an API or bulk access to our service. The tool is designed for individual use through the web interface. For commercial or bulk usage inquiries, please contact us directly.
Can I suggest new features?
Absolutely! We welcome feature suggestions. Email us at feedback@spanishwordunscrambler.com with your ideas. We review all suggestions and implement the most requested features when possible.
How can I support your project?
The best way to support us is to share Spanish Word Unscrambler with others who might find it useful. Word of mouth and social media shares help us reach more Spanish learners and word game enthusiasts.
Do you offer a Spanish version of this website?
We're working on a Spanish language version of the site. If you'd like to be notified when it's available, please email us and we'll add you to our notification list.
What information do you collect when I contact you?
When you email us, we only see the information you choose to include in your message (such as your email address and message content). We use this information solely to respond to your inquiry and don't share it with third parties. See our Privacy Policy for more details.
