How to Use Spanish Word Unscrambler

Complete step-by-step guide to unscrambling Spanish words

Quick Start Guide

1 Enter Your Letters

Type the scrambled Spanish letters into the input box. You can enter any combination of letters, including special Spanish characters like ñ, á, é, í, ó, ú.

2 Click "Unscramble Words"

Press the orange "Unscramble Words" button or hit Enter on your keyboard.

3 View Your Results

All possible Spanish words will appear instantly, sorted by length from longest to shortest.

4 Filter by Length (Optional)

Use the length filter dropdown to show only words of a specific length.

Examples

Example 1: Simple Word

Input: amor
Output: amor, roma, ramo, mora, armo

Example 2: With Special Characters

Input: españ
Output: España, añesp, peñas

Example 3: Longer Scramble

Input: palabar
Output: palabra, alabar, palaba

Tips for Best Results

  • Include All Letters: Enter all available letters, even if you think some won't be used
  • Try Variations: If you're not finding the word you need, try different letter combinations
  • Use Special Characters: Don't forget ñ and accented vowels are valid Spanish letters
  • Check Word Length: For crosswords, use the length filter to find words that fit specific spaces
  • Case Doesn't Matter: You can type in uppercase or lowercase

Typing Special Spanish Characters

On Windows:

  • ñ: Alt + 164 (or Alt + 0241)
  • á: Alt + 160
  • é: Alt + 130
  • í: Alt + 161
  • ó: Alt + 162
  • ú: Alt + 163

On Mac:

  • ñ: Option + n, then n
  • á: Option + e, then a
  • é: Option + e, then e
  • í: Option + e, then i
  • ó: Option + e, then o
  • ú: Option + e, then u

On Mobile: Long-press the letter to see accent options

Troubleshooting

No Results Found

If you're not getting results, check that:

  • All letters are spelled correctly
  • You have enough letters to form valid words
  • Special characters are typed correctly

Tool Not Responding

Try these solutions:

  • Refresh your browser page
  • Clear your browser cache
  • Try a different browser
  • Check your internet connection

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I save my results?

Currently, results are not saved. You can copy them to your clipboard or take a screenshot for future reference.

Is there a limit to how many letters I can enter?

No, you can enter any number of letters. However, very long combinations may produce fewer results.

Do accents matter in the search?

Yes and no. Our tool recognizes accented characters, but in most Spanish word games, accents don't affect validity.

Can I use this tool offline?

Once the page is loaded, the tool works in your browser and doesn't require an internet connection.
